About Kudla Kwasekhaya

Since

2010

Bringing the Taste of Home to You

Kudla Kwasekhaya means "Home Food" in siSwati. We are a family-owned restaurant born from a deep love for our land, our culture, and the traditional cooking methods passed down through generations.

Every dish we serve tells a story of Eswatini – from the fertile valleys of Malkerns to the cooking fires of our grandmothers. We use only organic, locally-sourced ingredients, prepared with time-honored techniques and a modern touch.
